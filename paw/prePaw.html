$pPAW

Pre-TGE PAW

Pre-TGE PAW ($pPAW) are non transferable tokens that are used to reward the community for interacting with BeraPaw and its partners. These tokens are redeemable 1:1 for $PAW at TGE, allowing users to convert their $pPAW into $PAW the official governance token of BeraPaw.

Total Supply: 15,000,000 (15% of $PAW total supply)

How to acquire $pPAW ?

Minting $LBGT

By minting $LBGT on any reward vault you will start accruing $pPAW. The amount of $pPAW you will receive is based on the amount of $LBGT minted. Holding $stLBGT

By holding $stLBGT in your wallet you will start accruing $pPAW. The amount of $pPAW you will receive is based on the amount of $stLBGT held and the time held. The longer you hold $stLBGT the more $pPAW you will receive. Note: If you stake the $stLBGT or use it somewhere else you will stop accruing $pPAW and will instead receive the yield/rewards of said protocol.

Interacting with Partners

Some partners interactions might be incentivized with $pPAW. These actions can be either temporary or permanent, be sure to check announcements and partners pages for more information.
